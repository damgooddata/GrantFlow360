# GrantFlow 360

**Streamline Your Grant Review Process from Application to Award.**

GrantFlow 360 is a comprehensive, cloud-based platform designed to revolutionize how foundations, non-profits, and educational institutions manage their grant review and funding cycles. Say goodbye to cumbersome spreadsheets, endless email chains, and administrative bottlenecks. Our intuitive portal provides a centralized hub for reviewers, administrators, and stakeholders to collaborate efficiently, make data-driven decisions, and award funding with confidence.

## The Problem

Managing a grant program involves juggling countless applicant files, coordinating feedback from multiple reviewers, and ensuring a fair, unbiased process. This often leads to administrative chaos, wasted time, and the risk of great applicants falling through the cracks.

## The Solution: GrantFlow 360

### How It Works: A Simple Workflow

1.  **Configure Your Cycle:** Administrators set up the grant cycle, define the review stages, and invite reviewers to the platform.
2.  **Review & Collaborate:** Reviewers log into their personalized dashboard. They can immediately filter applicants, dive into detailed profiles, and use integrated tools to leave comments and tag applicants.
3.  **Make Data-Driven Decisions:** Administrators use the reporting dashboard to monitor review progress in real-time. They analyze the applicant landscape with interactive charts and maps to identify standout candidates and ensure alignment with strategic goals.
4.  **Advance & Award:** Based on collaborative feedback and powerful data insights, administrators can confidently advance applicants through the rounds and select the final awardees.
